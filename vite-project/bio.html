TYLER McCOLLEY

Tyler McColley, a 25-year-old photographer from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, discovered his love for photography in the summer of 2015. Since then, he's become a sought-after visual artist, renowned for his attention to detail and knack for seizing life's fleeting moments. With a Bachelor's degree in graphic design from Hampton University, Tyler's unique blend of artistic sensibilities informs his photography. As the founder of TY.SHOTS, a venture he initiated during his college years, he's tirelessly built a reputation as a reliable and creative photographer, crafting captivating images that leave a lasting impact on his clients.
